About Me

< This is where I come to being >

Hello, thanks for visiting my space ! My name is Anup, and I'm a AI/ML Computer Performance Architect by professsion. I was born and raised in Guwahati, a city in the North-Eastern state of Assam in India. Surrounded by mighty Brahmaputra river at one end and hills on the other, this city has a trademark characteristic of an Indian small town that taught me to always have bigger aspirations in life.

My childhood memories are cherished by attending legacy Cotton Collegiate Govt Higher Secondary School followed by Cotton College. Since my childhood I had a deep internal desire to learn about the workings of a Computer (which was primarly used for playing the legendary RoadRash video game back in the 90's) - you could call it the life's calling that I was lucky to feel the pull at a very young age. Subsequently I completed my undergraduate studies in Electronics and Instrumentation Engineering from the National Institue of Technology, Rourkela in India. Following my Bachelors degree I have worked as an Automotive Electronics Engineer at Mahindra & Mahindra ltd, in Chennai.

As a next step to further my horizons, I decided to pursue higher studies in the land of opportunities and joined the Pennylvania State University as a graduate student, in the lovely town of State-College also called the Happy Valley. After spending about six and a half years at the Computer Science & Engineering Department, I've recently moved to San Jose in California to start my full time career. I'm grateful to all my family members and friends, who have helped to come this far in the journey of life.
